Word Link App - Chat Log

1.  Initial build (2026-03-12)
    A.  Daily puzzle game, 3 puzzles per day.
    B.  Two clue words given, player finds
        the linking word.
    C.  Tracks correct/incorrect and solve
        time.
    D.  Score from accuracy and speed.

2.  Nomenclature rename (2026-03-14)
    A.  Renamed all methods, variables,
        element IDs, and CSS classes.
    B.  Pattern: `concept[_modifier][_verb]`.
    C.  Snake case, lowercase throughout.
    D.  Related code shares a concept prefix.

3.  Key concepts (~20 domain nouns)
    A.  `puzzle`: single puzzle (two clues
        plus one link word).
    B.  `puzzle_set`: full collection of all
        221 puzzles.
    C.  `game`: today's session of 3 puzzles.
    D.  `clue`: hint word shown to player.
    E.  `guess`: user's submitted answer.
    F.  `attempt`: remaining tries per puzzle
        (starts at 2).
    G.  `timer`: elapsed time tracking.
    H.  `score`: points earned.
    I.  `stats`: cumulative player statistics
        (played, streak, avg).
    J.  `streak`: consecutive days played.
    K.  `progress`: dot indicators for puzzle
        completion.
    L.  `result`: outcome of one puzzle
        (correct/incorrect plus time).
    M.  `results`: final summary screen and
        breakdown.
    N.  `feedback`: message shown after a
        guess.
    O.  `state`: persisted session data in
        localStorage.
    P.  `date`: today's date key
        (YYYY-MM-DD).
    Q.  `card`: puzzle display container.
    R.  `input`: text entry field.
    S.  `button`: action trigger.

4.  Verbs (consistent across concepts)
    A.  `load`: read from localStorage.
    B.  `save`: write to localStorage.
    C.  `show`: render/display on screen.
    D.  `start`: begin (e.g. timer).
    E.  `stop`: halt (e.g. timer).
    F.  `update`: refresh a display element.
    G.  `calculate`: compute a value.
    H.  `submit`: send user input for
        evaluation.
    I.  `advance`: move to next puzzle.
    J.  `reset`: clear state and restart.
    K.  `select`: pick/choose (e.g. today's
        puzzles).
    L.  `get`: derive/retrieve a value.
