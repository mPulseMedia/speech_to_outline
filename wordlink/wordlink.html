How to Play

1

Each day you get three puzzles — Easy, Medium, and Hard.

2

Each puzzle shows two clue words. Find the single word that links both clues.

3

Guess as many times as you like. Fewer guesses = higher score.

4

Stuck? Use the Reveal Answer button, but you won't earn points for that puzzle.

Example
Pummel & Rhythm
Answer: Beat
